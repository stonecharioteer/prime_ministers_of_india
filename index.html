The Prime Ministers of India

A Study of the Indians who have held the Office of Prime Minister

Preliminary Study

In early December 2017, I began gathering data on the present and past Prime Ministers of India to get a better understanding of the people who have held that office. I wanted to do this to understand their history, the timeline of the office, and a little about how the office itself changed over time.

Data Arrangement

In this first graph, I've used the same idea as the original Reddit post I took inspiration from. The vertical axis denotes the prime ministers (both acting or otherwise), arranged according to their terms, and the horizontal axis indicates their age. This allows us to see at what point of their lives these people were in office.

Lifespans

Comparison of lifespans, showing when during their lifespans, they held office.

Tenure and Timeline

Digging In

After understanding the juncture in their lives in which they attained office, it is interesting to note which of the Prime Ministers were alive during each others' tenures. It helps us gain perspective on their policies, and understand how the office evolved.

Data Arrangement

This graph adds to the previous one: now the horizontal axis denotes the year and naturally, each horizontal bar is offset according to the Prime Ministers' birth date.
